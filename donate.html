Your Impact

What Your Gift Does

Every contribution — large or small — is directly invested in the holistic development of registered children and youth in Kitui County, Kenya.

$10
Provides nutritious meals for a child for one week, supporting physical development and school attendance.
$25
Covers school materials and stationery for one beneficiary for an entire term.
$50
Funds a medical check-up and treatment for one child during our outreach clinics.
$100
Sponsors a youth's vocational training module at Kenya Water Institute or an equivalent institution.
$500
Fully sponsors one child for an entire year — covering education, healthcare, nutrition, and spiritual development.

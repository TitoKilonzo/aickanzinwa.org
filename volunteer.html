Why Volunteer

Make a Real Difference on the Ground

Volunteering with AIC Kanzinwa CYDC is one of the most direct ways to contribute to holistic child development. Whether you are on-site in Kitui County or supporting us remotely, your skills and time create lasting change.

We welcome individuals, teams, and organisations who share our commitment to releasing children and youth from poverty in Jesus' Name.

Teaching & Tutoring

Support academic sessions, literacy programmes, and vocational education workshops.

Health & Medical

Assist medical outreach clinics, nutrition assessments, and health education campaigns.

Media & Comms

Document impact stories, manage social media, and create fundraising content.

Digital & Admin

Data entry, grant writing, website support, and programme administration.

Environmental

Tree planting, environmental education, and community clean-up initiatives.

Sports & Recreation

Coach sports teams, lead recreational activities, and facilitate team-building events.

Location

Kanzinwa Village, Mumoni Sub-county, Kitui County, Kenya — and also remote/online support opportunities available globally.

What to Expect

The Volunteer Journey

1. Apply

Submit the application form with your details and area of interest.

2. Interview

We connect with you to discuss your role, expectations, and programme fit.

3. Onboard

Receive an orientation, programme introduction, and safety briefing.

4. Serve

Begin your service and directly impact the lives of children and families.

Another Way to Help

Can't Volunteer? You Can Still Give.

Financial support is equally transformative — every contribution funds education, healthcare, and life-skills training for our beneficiaries.

The People Behind the Mission

Our team of committed professionals and church leaders work tirelessly to ensure every child in our programmes receives holistic, life-changing support.

Project Patron & Lead Pastor

Pastor Peter Musyoka

Project Patron — AIC Kanzinwa Church

Pastor Peter Musyoka is the Lead Pastor of Africa Inland Church Kanzinwa and the Patron of the Compassion International-supported AIC Kanzinwa Child Development Centre. Under his spiritual leadership, the project has grown from 200 to 371 registered beneficiaries and expanded to include the AIC Maeni Satellite. He provides the pastoral oversight, community trust, and theological grounding that anchors every programme.

Diploma in Theology — Scott Christian University
Lead Pastor — Africa Inland Church Kanzinwa
Compassion International Project Patron since 2009
Project Director

Joshua K. Mutisya

Project Director

Joshua leads the day-to-day operations of AIC Kanzinwa CYDC. He previously served as Project Accountant from March 2010 to December 2022, bringing deep institutional knowledge and a growing passion for technology-driven development.

Master of Development Studies — Mount Kenya University
Bachelor of Development Studies — Mount Kenya University
Diploma, Project Planning & Management — Rural Aid Training Institute
CPA — North Eastern National Polytechnic
Health & Social Worker

Fidelis Kaluki

Project Health Worker & Social Worker

A Registered Nurse licensed by the Nursing Council of Kenya, Fidelis delivers compassionate, child-centred care across the programme. She leads health education, medical outreach coordination, and social-emotional support — promoting the holistic well-being of every participant.

Diploma in Community Health Nursing — Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC)
Registered Nurse — Nursing Council of Kenya
Expertise in patient care, health education & community outreach
Project Accountant

Daudi Maanda Ngunga

Project Accountant

Daudi manages the project's financial records with precision and integrity, ensuring full compliance with accounting standards and providing financial insights that support sound decision-making across all programme areas.

Bachelor's Degree in Accounting
Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
Expert in financial management, auditing & tax regulations
Project Chairperson

Project Committee Chairperson

Project Committee Treasurer

The People Behind the Mission

Our team of committed professionals and church leaders work tirelessly to ensure every child in our programmes receives holistic, life-changing support.

Project Patron & Lead Pastor

Pastor Peter Musyoka

Patron — Africa Inland Church Kanzinwa

Pastor Peter Musyoka is the Lead Pastor of AIC Kanzinwa and the Patron of the Compassion International-supported KE0759 project. Under his spiritual leadership the CDC has grown from 200 to 371 beneficiaries, launched the AIC Maeni Satellite, and established the AIC Kanzinwa Academy — now ranked top 10 in Kitui County. He chairs the Overall Management Committee and provides the pastoral oversight, community trust, and theological grounding that anchors every programme.

Diploma in Theology — Scott Christian University
Lead Pastor — Africa Inland Church Kanzinwa since establishment
Patron & Overall Management Committee Chair — KE0759
Project Director

Joshua K. Mutisya

Project Director

Joshua leads the day-to-day operations of AIC Kanzinwa CYDC and serves as Secretary to the Overall Management Committee. He previously served as Project Accountant from March 2010 to December 2022, bringing deep institutional knowledge and a passion for community-driven development.

Master of Development Studies — Mount Kenya University
Bachelor of Development Studies — Mount Kenya University
Diploma in Project Planning & Management — Rural Aid Training Institute
CPA — North Eastern National Polytechnic
Health & Social Worker

Fidelis Kaluki

Project Health Worker & Social Worker

A Registered Nurse licensed by the Nursing Council of Kenya, Fidelis delivers compassionate, child-centred care across the programme. She leads health education, medical outreach coordination, and social-emotional support — promoting the holistic well-being of every participant.

Diploma in Community Health Nursing — KMTC
Registered Nurse — Nursing Council of Kenya
Community health, outreach & psychosocial support
Project Accountant

Daudi Maanda Ngunga

Project Accountant

Daudi manages the project's financial records with precision and integrity, ensuring full compliance with accounting standards. His work supports the audit excellence that has seen the project achieve a 90% Compassion International audit rating.

Bachelor's Degree in Accounting
Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
Financial management, auditing & compliance

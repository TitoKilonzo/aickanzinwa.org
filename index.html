Who We Are

Holistic Development for Every Child

Located in Kanzinwa village, Kitui County, Kenya, the Africa Inland Church Kanzinwa CDC has partnered with Compassion International since November 2008 to holistically release children and youth from poverty.

We serve registered beneficiaries across the Integrated Programme Module — covering survival, home-based, and sponsorship programmes — focusing on four core development pillars.

Spiritual Development — Commitment to the Lordship of Christ
Economic Development — Skills and self-reliance training
Socio-emotional Development — Compassion and community
Physical Development — Health and wellness practices

What We Do

Our Programmes

Through the Integrated Programme Module, we deliver life-changing programmes tailored to each child's needs — from infancy through to young adulthood.

Survival Programme

Nutrition, healthcare, and early childhood support for infants and toddlers in vulnerable households from birth to age 3.

Home-Based Care

Caregiver support, household empowerment, and family-centred interventions to strengthen the home environment.

Sponsorship Programme

Long-term one-to-one sponsorship covering education, healthcare, and spiritual nurture for children aged 3–22 years.

Medical Outreach

Periodic health clinics in partnership with Medical Missions Africa, providing free checkups, treatment, and health education.

Vocational Training

Skills training in plumbing, driving, and trade crafts — equipping youth with income-generating capabilities.

Child Protection

Community barazas, child neglect intervention, and anti-drug advocacy in partnership with government stakeholders.

Our Impact

From Wayua Karumba completing university to Daniel Muthoka earning his plumbing certification — each success story reflects the power of sustained, compassionate support.

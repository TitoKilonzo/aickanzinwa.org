Who We Are

Holistic Development for Every Child

AIC Kanzinwa operates a three-in-one model — the Church, the KE0759 Child Development Centre supported by Compassion International, and the AIC Kanzinwa Academy providing quality primary education. Together, they form one unified institution serving the most vulnerable children in Mumoni Sub-county, Kitui County.

Since our CDC commenced operations in January 2009, we have grown from 200 to 371 registered beneficiaries, launched the AIC Maeni Satellite, established a boarding academy ranked in the top 10 in Kitui County, and won multiple national Compassion Partner of the Year Awards.

What We Do

Our Programmes

Through the Integrated Programme Module, we deliver life-changing programmes from infancy through young adulthood — under three integrated pillars.

Survival Programme

Nutrition, healthcare, and early childhood support for infants and toddlers from birth to age 3, including support for expectant mothers.

Home-Based Care

Caregiver empowerment, household assessments, and family-centred interventions to strengthen the home environment.

Sponsorship Programme

Long-term one-to-one sponsorship covering education, healthcare, and spiritual nurture for children aged 3–22 years.

AIC Kanzinwa Academy

A church-based primary school with 271 pupils and 101 boarders — ranked top 10 in Kitui County (2023) and best in Mumoni Sub-county.

Vocational Training

Skills training in plumbing, driving, welding, and trades — equipping youth with income-generating capabilities for lasting independence.

Child Protection

Community barazas, Linda Mtoto SHG, drug abuse intervention, and anti-neglect advocacy in partnership with NGAO and local government.

Our Impact

Transforming Lives, One Child at a Time

From Wayua Karumba completing university to Daniel Muthoka and Titus Musyoki earning their plumbing and pipe fitting certifications at the Kenya Water Institute — our alumni are thriving across 29 counties in Kenya.

Our Compassion International audit score reached 90% in 2021, and we have won the Partner of the Year Award at both Regional and National level multiple times since 2015.

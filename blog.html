Building Hope Through Community Action: The AIC Maeni Classroom Project

For several years, children at AIC Maeni Satellite attended their Saturday programming sessions inside the church building — a space that was too small, congested, and frequently disrupted. Through unity, faith, and collective action, the community came together to construct a dedicated classroom. This is a testament to what shared purpose can accomplish.

AIC Kanzinwa Partners with NGAO to Address Drug Abuse and Child Neglect

In a collaborative effort, AIC Kanzinwa CYDC partnered with the National Government Administration Office (NGAO) to organise a public baraza in a community identified as a drug abuse hotspot. Approximately 70% of community members were involved in selling illicit brew. The baraza, spearheaded by child neglect champions, sought to protect children and restore family stability.

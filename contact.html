Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to AIC Kanzinwa CYDC?
You can donate through our Donate page, or contact us at ke759aickanzinwacdc@gmail.com for wire transfer and M-Pesa options. To directly sponsor a child, visit compassion.com and search for project KE0759.
How many children does the project support?
We currently serve 371 registered beneficiaries under the Integrated Programme Module (IPM), including survival, home-based, and sponsorship programmes — plus 30 additional beneficiaries at our AIC Maeni Satellite.
Can I volunteer at AIC Kanzinwa?
Yes! We welcome on-site volunteers in Kitui County, Kenya, as well as remote and online volunteers. Visit our Volunteer page to apply and learn about available roles.
What age group does the project serve?
We support children and youth from birth (survival programme) up to the age of 22, which is our maximum termination age. The sponsorship programme covers ages 3–22 years.
What is the relationship with Compassion International?
AIC Kanzinwa CYDC has been a Compassion International partner since 8th November 2008 under project code KE0759. Compassion International provides funding, programme frameworks, and accountability standards that guide our work.
Where exactly is the project located?
Our main centre is located in Kanzinwa village, Mbarani Sub-location, Katse Location, Mumoni Sub-county, Mwingi North Constituency, Kitui County, Kenya. Our satellite operates at AIC Maeni within a 5km radius.
